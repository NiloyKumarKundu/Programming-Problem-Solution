Problem:
---------
c = 5
a ^ b ^ c = 10
now find (a ^ b) = ?

solve:
    a^b^c = 10
=>  a^b^c^c = 10^c 
=>  a^b^0   = 10^5
=>  a^b     = 15
so ans = 15.

another way:
    c = 5 =         101
    a^b^c = 10 =    1010
    now 101^1010 = 1111 = 15 ans.
